Works / About / Instagram / Flickr

Photographer and maker, I engage with landscapes by examining their cultural misconceptions, nature-human relationships, and the ambivalent responses produced when we cannot comprehend them.

Most of my fieldwork involves working in the peripheries of urban and rural territories, specifically in transitional areas known as "edgelands". Ambiguous territories where self-seeding autonomous nature intersects with the discarded remnants of modern-day society among the layers of human narratives that have accumulated into the land over time.

BA (Hons) Lens based media, Lincoln College (Hull), 1:1 (2015/2018)
