rjcalow photography

I'm a photographer and maker exploring how we perceive landscapes—particularly where human and natural worlds blur.

My work often takes place in "edgelands"—transitional zones where wild growth overtakes forgotten infrastructure, revealing layered stories of use, neglect, and regeneration.
