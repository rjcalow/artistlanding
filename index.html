About

As an artist-photographer, I engage with misunderstood and ambivalent landscapes with an interwoven nature-human relationship.

As a maker, I employ an eclectic array of traditional, contemporary and alternative photographic practices led by narrative and conceptual themes. 


2019/2020 MA Photography at Nottingham Trent University. 1:1.
2015/18 - BA (Hons) Lens based media at Lincoln College (Hull) 1:1.
